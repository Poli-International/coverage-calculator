⚠️ Important Disclaimers

Coverage and Time Estimates: All calculations are based on industry averages and may vary significantly based on individual artist skill, technique, client skin type, design complexity, and other factors. Always use professional judgment and adjust estimates based on your experience.

Pricing Guidance: Pricing recommendations are for reference only. Actual pricing should consider your local market, overhead costs, experience level, and business model. This tool does not provide financial advice.

Ink Consumption: Ink estimates include typical waste factors but may vary based on technique, equipment, and artist efficiency.

Client Safety: Always prioritize client wellbeing over speed. Some clients may require shorter or longer sessions than estimated. Watch for signs of fatigue, pain, or shock.
