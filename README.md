## 🎯 Overview

The Tattoo Aftercare & Coverage Calculator helps clients and artists determine the best aftercare routine based on the size and location of a tattoo. It provides detailed timelines for healing and ensures that clients have the right amount of aftercare products for their new ink.

**Key Use Cases:**

- Planning aftercare product volume
- Visualizing healing milestones
- Customizing routines based on skin type and placement

**Target Audience:**

- Tattoo Artists (for client education)
- Clients (for recovery planning)

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Area-Based Calculations** - Precise volume estimates for aftercare creams and films.
- ✅ **Healing Timelines** - Visual interactive guide to the recovery process.
- ✅ **Custom Recommendations** - Advice tailored to specific body placements.
- ✅ **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- ✅ **Free & Open Source** - MIT licensed.
